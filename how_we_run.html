About Us

Ankur Rehab Center has come up with this novel idea of setting up Central India’s first & only 200 bed professional Psycho-Social Rehabilitation Center for catering addiction, psychiatric, psychological & old age problems by highly expertise degree holder professionals exclusively based on the specific need of the family members & their ward so that resident can experience the comfortable stay with quality care, kindness, love, empathy & security, as safe as the home environment along with rehabilitation program.
