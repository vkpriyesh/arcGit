Introduction

Ankur Rehab Center has come up with this novel idea of setting up Central India’s first & only 200 bed professional Psycho-Social Rehabilitation Center for catering addiction, psychiatric, psychological & old age problems by highly expertise degree holder professionals exclusively based on the specific need of the family members & their ward so that resident can experience the comfortable stay with quality care, kindness, love, empathy & security as safe as the home environment along with rehabilitation program. The easily approachable outskirt area away from city pollution & congestion spreads in about One Lac sq. ft. out of which only 18000 sq.ft. floor area is constructed (Total Constructed Area : 79,200) and being utilized for stay, rest adjacent area is surrounded by lush green, agricultural & horticultural farms & lands - one of the unique feature of the Center which provides very healthy, clean, green, calm, quite & stress free environment to the residents.

Requirement of Rehabilitation

Millions of lives have been ruined, injured, debilitated or/& ended. Careers and jobs have been lost, savings have been squandered, families have broken up and some have even lost everything. Many of the crimes involved mental illness or drugs. Many a times it is often committed to support their own drug habit. In a family, where less comprehending members find it as a curse and for those who have less understanding and lack of resources, this issue takes a mountainous proportion. In a nut-shell each and every family having mentally ill member needs extra support, care, treatment, medicine, guidance rehabilitation & re-structuring of their ward & to some extend for themselves also. The situation becomes worse where there is no care giver/taker, lack of facilities, family members or siblings are not interested in taking care for long duration, nuclear families and same such situations.
